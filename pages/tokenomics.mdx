### Circulating Supply (90%)
The majority of tokens are fairly launched and available to the community from day one, ensuring wide distribution and decentralized ownership. This approach promotes a fair and inclusive ecosystem where everyone has the opportunity to participate.

### Prize Pool & Treasury (5%)
This allocation is reserved for:
- Rewarding early participants who joined EAP
- Rewarding active participants in the Neur ecosystem
- Incentivizing community contributions
- Supporting ecosystem growth initiatives
- Funding future development and improvements

### Team (5%)
Allocated for the core team developing the Neur framework, ecosystem, and community. To ensure long-term alignment and prevent market disruption:
- Team allocations are locked in escrow
- Tokens vest linearly over a 1-year period
- Smart contract-enforced vesting schedule

## Vesting Schedule

To maintain price stability and ensure long-term commitment:
- Team tokens are subject to a 12-month linear vesting schedule
- Treasury funds are released gradually based on community governance decisions
- All vesting and lock-up mechanisms are enforced through smart contracts
